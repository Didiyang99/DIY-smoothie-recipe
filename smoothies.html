Smoothie [1]

A smoothie or smoothie is a drink made from pureed raw fruit and/or vegetables, using a blender.

Liquid base

  • fruit
  • juice
  • dairy
    • milk
    • yogurt
    • ice cream
    • cottage cheese
Smoothies may be made using other ingredients, such as non-dairy milk, crushed ice, sweeteners (honey or sugar), vinegar, whey powder, chocolate or nutritional supplements, among others by personal choice.
